About Us

We started our business in the capital city of Myanmar in 2002 and extend our business
by opening new branch offices and retail stores in three large city and deliver the product
around the country.

All the products from our store is designed by our experienced designers.
Our company usually participate in national trade shows.
We won the best innovative furniture designer of the year for three consecutive years
in 2011, 2012 and 2013.

We strike our best to expand our market into global environment.

Our future plan is to open our branch offices in south east aisa countries
and Australia in next five years.
